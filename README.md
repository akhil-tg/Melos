# Melos

Melos is a powerful monorepo management tool for Dart and Flutter projects. It simplifies workflows for managing dependencies, scripts, versioning, and publishing packages within a monorepo structure. With Melos, developers can efficiently organize their projects, ensuring a smoother development experience.

## Features of Melos
- **Dependency Management**: Easily manage package dependencies across your monorepo.
- **Scripting**: Run scripts in multiple packages simultaneously, saving time in the development process.
- **Versioning**: Automate versioning to maintain consistency across packages.
- **Publishing**: Publish packages with ease and confidence.

For more information, visit the [Melos documentation](https://melos.invertase.io).

# Musily

Musily is a music streaming application that offers users a unique listening experience. Built with a focus on user-friendly design and powerful features, Musily lets you discover new music, create playlists, and enjoy a personalized music experience.

## Features of Musily
- **Stream High-Quality Music**: Access a vast library of songs in high definition.
- **Personalized Playlists**: Create and customize playlists tailored to your musical taste.
- **Discover New Artists**: Explore and discover new music through curated recommendations.
- **Offline Play**: Download your favorite tracks and listen offline.

Together, Melos and Musily showcase innovative solutions for developers and music lovers alike, demonstrating the power of modern application development and user-centric design.
